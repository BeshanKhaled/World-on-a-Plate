Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my own recipe?

We love featuring community recipes! Send us your recipe with photos and story to recipes@worldonaplate.com. Our team will review and potentially feature it on our site.

Are all recipes tested?

Yes! Every recipe on our site is tested multiple times by our team of chefs to ensure accuracy and delicious results.

Can I request a specific recipe?

Absolutely! We're always looking for new recipes to feature. Send us your request and we'll do our best to find or create the perfect recipe for you.

Do you offer cooking classes?

We're working on launching virtual cooking classes! Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when they're available.
