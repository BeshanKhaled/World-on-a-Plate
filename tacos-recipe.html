Tacos

Soft Corn Tortillas Filled with Seasoned Meat and Fresh Toppings

Easy 20 min 4 servings Mexican

Tacos are a beloved Mexican street food that has become popular worldwide. This recipe features soft corn tortillas filled with flavorful seasoned ground beef, topped with fresh vegetables, cheese, and a variety of salsas and condiments for a complete and satisfying meal.

Share this recipe:

Ingredients

For the Meat Filling:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

For the Tortillas:

  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil for heating

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Hot sauce or salsa

  • 1 avocado, sliced

Instructions

1

Prepare the Meat Filling

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.

2

Cook the Ground Beef

Add ground beef to the skillet and break it up with a wooden spoon. Cook until browned, about 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3

Season the Meat

Stir in taco seasoning, water, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the liquid is mostly absorbed and flavors are well combined.

4

Warm the Tortillas

Heat a small amount of vegetable oil in a separate skillet over medium heat. Warm each tortilla for about 30 seconds on each side until soft and pliable. Keep warm in a clean kitchen towel.

5

Assemble the Tacos

Place a warm tortilla on a plate. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the meat mixture in the center. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and other desired toppings.

6

Serve and Enjoy

Fold the tortilla in half and serve immediately. Accompany with lime wedges, hot sauce, and additional toppings on the side.
